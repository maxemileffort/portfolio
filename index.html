Woody the Dev

Coding outside the proverbial box

Hi, I'm Max, a Full Stack Web Developer.

Technology is my jam! I have been into computers ever since I was a little kid. My dad helped me build my first one, which was mostly hand-me down parts from his own computer as he upgraded it.

Still, I remember how excited I would get whenever his new parts showed up, because it meant I would get to watch him crack open his PC and tinker a bit, and then we got to do the same thing to my Frankenstein machine!

I've been a tinkerer ever since, and I've been programming since 2017. I've mostly focused on Web Development, but I've also dabbled a bit in data science and even a little robotics. When I'm not coding, you can usually find me at the gym training BJJ, or playing with my kids.

Take a look at my projects, and if you're interested, give those contact buttons at the bottom some love.
